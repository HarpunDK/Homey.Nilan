Adds support for Nilan Compact P - Air9

With LAN-connected Nilan device, this app will read values and add them to Homey. 
Network-settings can be configured in the device settings.

Values that are read from the device: outside temp., supply temp., condensator temp. hot water temperature and more. 
Also days till next filter change of inlet and outlet filters are added as well.

This app brings conditions to your flow with support for inlet-/outlet-filter needs service and outside temperature sensor condition as well.
